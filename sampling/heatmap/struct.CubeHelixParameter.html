Implements color palett from https://arxiv.org/abs/1108.5083

Whatโs so good about this palett? It is monotonically increasing in perceived brightness. That means, it is well suited for being printed in black and white.

use sampling::heatmap::*;
let mut params = CubeHelixParameter::default();
params.rotation(1.3)
        .gamma(1.1)
        .start_color(0.3)
        .reverse(true);
